Start with Heart

How to stay focused on what you really want
Part I — Before You Open Your Mouth

Key Concepts

Start with Heart
Before opening your mouth, clarify what you really want—for yourself, for the other person, and for the relationship.
The Fool’s Choice
The false belief that you must choose between honesty and kindness. Skilled people refuse this trade-off.
Motive Check
Ask: ‘What am I acting like I want?’ vs. ‘What do I really want?’
Refocus
When emotions spike, pause and return to your original goal.

Discussion Question

Whole-Group Discussion

Describe a time you fell into the Fool’s Choice—choosing silence to keep peace, or aggression to make a point. What would ‘refusing the Fool’s Choice’ have looked like?

Personal Reflection

  1. What do you really want from an upcoming difficult conversation?
  2. What do you want for the other person?
  3. What do you want for the relationship?
