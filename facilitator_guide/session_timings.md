# Facilitator Guide - Session Timings

## Overview

This guide provides recommended timing for each chapter presentation, including breaks, activities, and discussion periods. Adjust based on your group's needs and experience level.

## Session Formats

### Full-Day Workshop (8 hours)
- **Morning Session**: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM (3 hours)
- **Lunch Break**: 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM (1 hour)
- **Afternoon Session**: 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM (4 hours)

### Half-Day Workshop (4 hours)
- **Session**: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM or 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM

### Weekly Sessions (1.5 hours each)
- **12-week program**: Cover 1-2 chapters per session

## Activity Timing Guidelines

### Types of Activities

#### Role-Playing Exercises
- **Preparation**: 5 minutes
- **Role-play**: 10-15 minutes
- **Debrief**: 10 minutes
- **Total**: 25-30 minutes

#### Small Group Discussions
- **Instructions**: 2 minutes
- **Discussion**: 15-20 minutes
- **Report-out**: 10 minutes
- **Total**: 27-32 minutes

#### Individual Reflection
- **Prompt**: 2 minutes
- **Writing**: 10-15 minutes
- **Sharing**: 10 minutes
- **Total**: 22-27 minutes

#### Video Clips
- **Introduction**: 2 minutes
- **Video**: 5-10 minutes
- **Discussion**: 15-20 minutes
- **Total**: 22-32 minutes

## Break Recommendations

### Standard Breaks
- **15-minute breaks** every 90-120 minutes
- **Longer breaks** (20-30 minutes) for meals

### Break Activities
- Stretching and movement
- Informal networking
- Resource browsing
- Question submission box

## Flexibility Guidelines

### When to Adjust Timing

#### Extend Discussion When:
- High participant engagement
- Complex real-world issues emerge
- Group needs more processing time

#### Shorten Activities When:
- Running behind schedule
- Group grasps concepts quickly
- Energy levels are low

#### Skip Activities When:
- Severe time constraints
- Group already mastered concepts
- Technical difficulties

### Time Management Tips

1. **Use a visible timer** for activities
2. **Assign a timekeeper** from the group
3. **Build in buffer time** (5-10 minutes per session)
4. **Have optional activities** ready for extra time
5. **Know which content can be shortened** if needed

## Materials Preparation

### For Each Session
- Chapter slides loaded and tested
- Handouts printed and organized
- Activity materials prepared
- Evaluation forms ready

### Technology Setup
- Projector and screen tested
- Audio system checked
- Internet connectivity verified
