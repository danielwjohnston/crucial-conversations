3 How to Finish

Retake Your Pen CURE

Hear almost anything

Collect yourself • Understand • Recover • Engage. Find the gold, discard the rest.

Move to Action WWWF

Decide & follow through

Choose method (Command, Consult, Vote, Consensus). Document: Who does What by When + Follow up.
