Chapter 10 Handout

Retake Your Pen

How to be resilient and hear almost anything
Part II — How to Open Your Mouth

Key Concepts

Retake Your Pen
You are the author of your own story. Don’t let others write it for you.
Separate Intent from Impact
Just because something hurt doesn’t mean harm was intended.
Natural Consequences
Let people experience the natural outcomes of their choices instead of shielding or punishing.
Resilience
The ability to hear hard feedback without losing your sense of self.

Discussion Question

Whole-Group Discussion

When has someone’s feedback felt like an attack? Looking back, were they writing your story—or were you letting them? How can you ‘retake your pen’ in future conversations?

Personal Reflection

  1. Think of feedback that stung. What story did you write about yourself because of it?
  2. How would you rewrite that story with more agency?
  3. What’s one area where you’ve given away your pen?
