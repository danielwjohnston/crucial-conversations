Putting It All Together

Tools for preparing and learning
Part III — How to Finish

Key Concepts

Coaching Model
Prepare → Practice → Perform → Review. Skill-building is iterative.
Preparation Worksheet
Before a crucial conversation: clarify your purpose, gather facts, plan your STATE, anticipate their path.
Two-Minute Drill
Mentally rehearse the first two minutes of the conversation—the opening sets the tone.
Lifelong Practice
Mastery comes from repeated application, not one-time reading.

Discussion Question

Whole-Group Discussion

Looking back at this entire course, which single skill or concept will make the biggest difference in your life? How will you practice it this week?

Personal Reflection

  1. Rank the skills from this course: which do you do best? Which needs the most work?
  2. Write out a preparation plan for your next crucial conversation.
  3. Who will be your accountability partner for practicing these skills?
